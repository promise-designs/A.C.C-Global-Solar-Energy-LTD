About A.C C Global Solar

A.C C Global Solar Energy Ltd is a trusted and registered dealer in all kinds of solar energy systems in Nigeria. Established in Alaba International Market, Ojo, Lagos State, we have built a solid reputation for providing quality solar products, professional installation services, and reliable after-sales maintenance to homes and businesses across the country.

Our mission is to make clean, renewable energy accessible and affordable to every Nigerian. We stock a wide range of solar panels, inverters, batteries, charge controllers, generators, freezers, and rechargeable fans — all sourced from reputable manufacturers. Whether you need a small home setup or a large commercial installation, we have the expertise and products to power your needs.

  • Quality Solar Products at Affordable Prices
  • Professional Sales, Installation & Maintenance
  • Nationwide Delivery to All 36 States + FCT
  • Trusted Dealer at Alaba International Market
